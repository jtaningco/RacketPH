About Racket

Our Story

Racket was conceptualized by 4 young college students who were stuck at home in the middle of a global pandemic. They noticed that employees were being laid off in the hundreds, and freelancers were losing projects quick. With this, the team set out to create a job platform addressing both issues: the sudden influx of unemployed professionals and the lack of exposure for freelancers. Thus, Racket was born.
