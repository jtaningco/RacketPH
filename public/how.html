How It Works

Freelancers Clients

Freelancers

All-Around Dashboard

Experience convenience and easy access to your content with our multi-functional dashboard! Access your profile, jobs, and portfolio effortlessly and see your success grow in real time!


With this, assemble your portfolio effortlessly with our built-in portfolio system! Organize your skills and achievements in an easy-to-digest manner for clients to see your skills, which will later be put to the test.

Guided Projects

Are you a beginner? Don’t know where to start? We got you! With our Guided Projects feature, we’ll be guiding you throughout the whole process towards the start of your freelance journey with the use of our industry-proven simulations!


Guided Projects allow you to experience the freelancing responsibility and workload, without the actual pressure! Check out tons of project templates you can choose from, and start on your journey to becoming a full-fledged professional!

Job Finding

Having a hard time looking for jobs matching you? Look no further! Search projects without the hassle with our Job Finding feature! Be partnered with clients aligned with your skills, and see yourself excel on the professional level!


Sit back and relax as our advanced artificial intelligence matching does the heavy lifting for you. Connect with clients fast, and watch as they flock over your profile and take your talents to heights never before reached.

Clients

AI Matching

Once you post a project, sit back and relax as our advanced matching system does the work for you. Match with freelancers suited perfectly for your needs, and see your business grow like never before!


Our advanced matching system takes the freelancer’s skillset, interests, and previous clients into consideration, all in providing you the best possible partners in taking your project to greater heights.

Communications System

Connect and negotiate with freelancers using our built-in communications system. Relay information, send documents, and do so much more with one of Racket’s many premier features.


Stay professional and up to speed with our push notification system, informing you with news from freelancers expressing interest to your posted projects, to in project activities.

Guaranteed Delivery

Output wasn’t what you wanted? Remove the risk of paying for unsatisfactory work with our escrow payment method! Deposit your payment via a third-party partner, and pay only when the work meets your expectations!
